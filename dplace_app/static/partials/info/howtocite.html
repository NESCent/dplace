How to cite

Research that uses data from D-PLACE should cite both the original source(s) of the data and this paper (e.g., research using cultural data from the Binford Hunter-Gatherer dataset: “Binford (2001); Binford and Johnson (2006); Kirby et al. Submitted).” The reference list should include the date data were accessed and URL for D-PLACE (http://d-place.org), in addition to the full references for Binford (2001) and Binford and Johnson (2006).
